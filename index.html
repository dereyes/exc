Building Sustainable Retail Together

Synchronization and Syndication

Exchange Collective imports product and inventory data from brands, big and small, across many industries. We then synchronize that data with the companies/brands real time inventory feed. We then syndicate that data to our retailers eCommerce platform, and/or in-store app.

Fulfillment and Order Management

When an order is placed, it is routed to the vendor, and then shipped direct to the customer. Once the order is fulfilled, the tracking information flows back to the retailer, and a notification is sent to the customer as well.

Invoicing, Billing and Payment

Exchange Collective is the master account for all drop shipped orders. Exchange Collective receives payment at the time the order is placed. The brand then invoices Exchange Collective once the order has shipped. Exchange Collective then pays the invoice from the brand and the net profit to the retailer.
